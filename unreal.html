Game Engines to use in 2026

Unreal Engine

Unreal Engine is an open source, free to download game engine, developed by Epic games that is very standard amongst triple A developers.

The Good

  1. Unreal is an industry standard. Potential employers will appreciate your Unreal engine expertise
  2. Unreal is known for it's stunnng graphics. It has very high graphics capabilities and excellent graphics tools
  3. Optimised for high performance. It's great for making games that run smoothly

The Bad

  1. Unreal has a steep learning curve and it takes time to master it's features
  2. Unreal is a commercial product, so you will need to pay a license in order to use it
  3. Unreal has limited cross platform support, making it difficult to make games that run seamlessly on all platforms

Games made in Unreal

Numerous games have been made in this engine, such as;

Avowed

Avowed is a fantasy RPG game released by Obsidian entertainment in 2025. You navigate a wartorn fantasy land where you choices carve the path

Fortnite

Fortnite is an online battle royale that took the world by storm in 2017. You deploy on to an island with 99 other players and scavange the map for weapons and items. The goal is to be the last player standing.

Tekken 8

The eighth installment in the long running 1v1 fighter series, Tekken, was developed in Unreal Engine and released in 2024. With 32 playable characters, the game is touted as "the biggest game in the series"
